4D Decomposition Principle: The system of 4 hyperplanes is decomposed into four triplets (groups of 3 equations). For any given X coordinate on the axis, each triplet creates a system of 3 equations with 3 unknowns (Y, Z, W).

On the 1D canvas, 4 horizontal strips are shown: each representing the solution space of one equation triplet. The resulting Y, Z, and W coordinates are mapped to the Red, Green, and Blue channels respectively.

If a solution exists for the full 4x4 system, exactly at coordinate X, the colors of all 4 strips will perfectly align and mix, representing the common coordinate point in the 4-dimensional space.
